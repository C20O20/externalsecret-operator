1. Deploy an ExternalSecret CR in the namespace watched by the External Secret Operator
   using name provided in the configuration secret as Backend.

   apiVersion: externalsecret-operator.container-solutions.com/v1alpha1
   kind: ExternalSecret
   metadata:
    name: example-externalsecret-asm
   spec:
    Key: example-externalsecret-key
    Backend: asm-example
